Regras/Rules

PT

Use as setas arrow_back, arrow_downward, arrow_forward e arrow_upward ou arraste o dedo na tela para controlar a direção para onde os bloquinhos serão arremessados.

Quando dois bloquinhos de mesmo valor se chocam, eles se somam.

Seu objetivo é chocar os bloquinhos até chegar num bloquinho cujo valor seja 2048. Ou maior... ;)

EN

Use the keyboard arrows arrow_back, arrow_downward, arrow_forward and arrow_upward or slide your finger on the screen to define in which direction the tiles will be thrown.

When two tiles with the same value and color colide, they become a tile with double the previous value.

Your goal is to colide as many blocks you can to create bigger tiles until reaching 2048. Or even more... ;)
